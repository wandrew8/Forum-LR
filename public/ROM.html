ROM: Renewing Our Minds

Renewing Our Minds

ROM is a Southwest European initiative of change with a vision of a transformed leadership, inspired by the character and teachings of the person of Jesus. Its mission is to develop leaders of integrity, committed to reconciliation, peace-building and healing social action; leaders, who renewed in their mind, character, attitudes and actions in the likeness of Jesus, are steadily transforming their constituencies, communities and country.

Following the Balkan Wars (1991-1995) in Croatia and Bosnia-Herzegovina, a group of young adults associated with Life Center International (LCI) from Croatia, gave birth to the international ROM movement. ROM was born in response to prevailing ethnic, religious, political and economic distress; and at the time when an emerging generation of young leaders in the region lacked role models and a vision for the future of the post-war Balkan region. The first ROM Gathering was launched in 1999 in this beautiful township of Fuzine in Croatia. Over the years ROM has grown into an international family of friends that embraces young adults and emerging from 40 countries of the world. In 2011 ROM became part of a non-profit organization, Forum for Leadership and Reconciliation whose purpose is to develop leaders transformed by Jesus in a divided world.

ROM Gathering is a journey that begins with the question: “Who or what is impacting your mind?” Over the next two weeks you will be guided from one question to another, all of them leading you beyond the daily superficialities of life into pondering deep into the themes and subjects that matter and are true, defining, dignifying and meaningful. On that journey we are all fellow seekers, students and teachers at the same time.

Wherever you are coming from we welcome you with a big hug, as a friend and fellow traveler. The only requirement is that you come with an open heart and mind, desiring to encounter new friends and reach across new frontiers.

You are venturing in the realm of ROM at your own risk, we cannot guarantee that you will ever be the same again! Welcome to the adventure. We love you and wish you a life-changing and exciting journey.

Yours with open hearts,


On behalf of ROM Gathering Leadership Team
