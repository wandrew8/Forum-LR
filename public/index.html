Renewing Our Minds

The Renewing Our Minds Gathering (ROM) has been a defining and life changing experience for hundreds of young participants from over 50 countries. Many alumni have emerged as leaders of influence and are today making a difference in the political, social and humanitarian and religious arenas of their countries.

Economic Deplomacy and Integrity

Economic Diplomacy and Integrity (EDI) is designed to support professionals and students in the areas of economics, business and politics. We believe that the best way to fight against unhealthy rivalry among and within nations is to establish an economic and plitical community based on Jesus's life and principals.
